Lymington - Vodka and Cherryade (final edit)

Lymington - Vodka and Cherryade (complete) from Josh Pamfilo on Vimeo.

Here is the final edit of my music video for Lymington's song "Vodka and Cherryade". However, there are subtle differences between this edit and the other existing copies of this music video. Firstly, the introductory shot of the drummer getting equipment out of the car was replaced by a LEGO version of the vocalist raising his arm to the screen in stop motion. This provided a more fitting sequence of events.

Secondly, more LEGO stop-motion shots were added to replace less fitting shots that would have not complemented the music video. This, in turn, would balance out the live-action aspects with the stop-motion animation aspects.

Progress: Ancillary product shots (magazine advert)


The original, unedited image used for my magazine advert promoting Lymington's debut album "Vodka and Cherryade". In it, the BHS sign is still intact, along with the lamp post nearby before editing of the "cherry graffiti" motif in order to maintain brand image. I used my iPhone 6 to capture this brilliant image for the magazine advert as it provides high-definition pixels for quality assurance.


Sean's drum kit assembled and arranged, ready for their April gig. (April 22)
This image was used for the back cover of their album and the lighting enhances the graininess of the photo. It had to be cropped into a square in order to comply with correct measurements.

Wednesday, 21 December 2016

MUSIC VIDEO PROGRESS: LEGO Lymington

Lymington, only re-imagined in LEGO form. (l to r: Elliot, Leo, Sean, Freddy, James, Jake)
For certain cutaways in my final draft, I decided to follow a more conventional path: using stop-motion animation mechanics. This would fill in for any band close-ups and footage that I missed out on filming from last July during their Chinnerys performance. I built a drumkit for Sean to use for close-ups as well as given four band members guitars to focus on during filming. This was an innovative approach to filming live action music videos.
