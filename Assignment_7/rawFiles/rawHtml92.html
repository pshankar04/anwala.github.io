Mux242: Ten Years of Mux

Ten years ago this May, a short-lived website called muxtape.com introduced an easy, barebones way to share streamable mixes. It didn't last very long, but I kept making twice-monthly mixes based on a handful of hard-and-fast rules. Flash forward ten years, and I'm still making them with most of the same rules: a band can only have one track per mix; a band can't be on successive muxes; muxes have twelve tracks; and no song can ever be repeated.

To mark a decade of mux, I've compiled a mix of some of the most-muxed bands. And lo, the tracks for Mux242: Ten Years of Mux, plus the total number of muxed song by the featured bands:
