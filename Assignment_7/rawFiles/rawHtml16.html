Taking a break

Hey everyone,

Due to work and school, I have to stop hosting GE&H Monday mornings. It's been a fun 5 years hosting this show and I am already missing it. I will try to find an evening show time that will work better and will keep you posted on that. I am also in the process of getting my certification to lead group fitness classes, so I anticipate posting some sweet workout mixed on here in the near future.

Later gators.
