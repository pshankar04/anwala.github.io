Saturday, December 9, 2017

Thank You

88 art pieces that I have made.  2 art pieces done by separate individuals.  Combine that with several playlists and new music recommendations and I'd say you had a pretty successful experiment.  Though the overall goal of an online global art and music sharing community was never successfully reached due to poor exposure (my fault entirely) and lack of any (minus two) outside art submissions, I still feel very proud of this little achievement, even if it did just evolve into my own personal art show.

On this exact day last year, I began working on my very first piece for this site, made while listening to Everything But The Girl's underrated slow-burner "Amplified Heart".  About two weeks later, this site was up and running.  However, some things must come to an end, and I can comfortably say that there will be no more uploads to this blog.

I thank John Grant, Tanya Tagaq, the many members of The Pablo Collective, Jack Riordan, and the members of Shataan for giving me some kind words and recognition, and to whatever audience I may or may not have had with this little project.

Thank you and goodbye.

- Ross Allison

Get Your Artwork On Here!

  • The purpose of this blog is to show how music can directly effect artistic intuition. Each piece of art is made while listening to the duration of an album by any music artist. The works of art can then be considered separate album covers. Any artists interested in submitting can submit their works at ross24fps@gmail.com. When sending the picture file, be sure to also give your name (or artistic pseudonym if that's how you roll), materials used, height and width of the piece, and the name of the musician/band and the album you listened to as you made your piece.
