Wednesday, July 21, 2010

Love

Love

Love doesn’t know boundaries,

Love doesn’t own a place

Love knows no color,

Love knows no shape,

Love doesn’t hurt,

Love doesn’t own.

Love doesn’t know the value of money

Love knows what a forgotten heart is

Love is blind and lonely

Love stays the same even with time

Love yearns for forgiveness

Love is big,

Love is fat,

Love is sick,

Love is bad,

Love is crazy

Love is a fad,

Love is a dizzy

Love is mad

Love gives

Love takes

Love misses

Love gets

Love grabs,

Love will always be there and always will want…
